class  itc::RScheduler
 implements scheduling of tasks execution at specific timepoint. With this class it is possible to schedule a task to be executed within some miliseconds after call to RScheduler::add() method. The execution of a task is scheduled to happen at the specific time, however it may be delayed by 100 microseconds or a little longer. The delay over 100 microseconds depends on a granularity of a system timers. More...
